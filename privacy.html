Privacy Policy

1. Introduction

Welcome to Alris. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy outlines our practices regarding the collection, use, and protection of your information when you use our application.

2. Information We Collect

User Behavior Data: We collect information about your interactions with the app, such as features used, session duration, and other usage statistics. This data helps us understand how users engage with our app and identify areas for improvement.

3. Information We Do Not Collect

Photos and Media: We do not collect, upload, or store any photos or media from your device. All photo analysis and processing are performed locally on your device using on-device AI technology. Your photos remain private and are never transmitted to our servers or third-party services.

4. Use of Collected Information

The user behavior data we collect is used to:

  • Analyze and improve app functionality and user experience.
  • Identify and fix technical issues.
  • Develop new features and enhancements.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures to protect the information we collect. However, please be aware that no method of electronic transmission or storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Third-Party Services

Our app does not integrate with third-party services that collect or process your personal information.

7. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have inadvertently collected such information, we will take steps to delete it promptly.

8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted within the app, and the "Effective Date" at the top will be updated accordingly. We encourage you to review this policy periodically to stay informed about how we are protecting your information.

9. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:
